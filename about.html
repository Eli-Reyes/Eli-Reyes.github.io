This is me

Hi, I'm Eli Reyes, UX/UI designer.

I graduated in Market Research and Techniques, which gave me the opportunity to work in banking when I arrived in Madrid, going through different departments for 15 years.

What has this work experience given me? a lot, from learning to communicate with other departments, to being analytical, organized, autonomous, but at the same time flexible and with the ability to make decisions and work as a team. Being close to clients has taught me to know what “active listening” is and to empathize with them, giving them the most optimal solution based on their problems and needs.

But my passions have always been to discover, create and help.

My artistic interests and passions have always made me in contact with workshops, courses and art schools, changing my direction towards design. Going through Arte 10, where I specialized as an Architecture Model Maker, landing at Ironhack to expand my professional career by taking the UX/UI Design Bootcamp and fulfilling one of my dreams “to merge my professional training with my passion for design and research ” .

Proactive, curious, open to new challenges and wanting to continue learning and getting involved in everything I do.
